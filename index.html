FHIR end points

A list of public FHIR end points of US-based healthcare providers.

This list is currently maintained by CareEvolution Inc. It is the same list used internally by CareEvolution's myFHR patient app.

The data comes from vendors' published list like Epic and Cerner plus CareEvolution's own customers and other individual providers that made their information available. The vendors' lists are periodically imported and augmented with extra information like addresses and ZIP codes.

CareEvolution periodically checks all end points to see if they are still active and have a reachable login page, fix out of date end point URLs and report problems to the vendor and / or the provider.
